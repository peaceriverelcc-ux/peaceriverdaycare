Where Learning Flows Like a River

Welcome to Our Daycare!

At Peace River Early Learning, we are committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and engaging environment where children of all ages can learn, grow, and flourish. Our programs are thoughtfully designed to support every stage of early childhood development, blending educational activities with creative play to inspire curiosity, confidence, and a lifelong love of learning.

Our dedicated team of educators works closely with families to ensure each child feels supported, valued, and encouraged to reach their full potential. Whether your child is just beginning their early learning journey or is preparing for school, we’re here to offer compassionate care, meaningful experiences, and a strong foundation for the future.

Why Choose Us?

  • Qualified Teachers - Our experienced staff are certified in early childhood education and are dedicated to nurturing every child's development.
  • Safe Environment - Your child's safety is our top priority. Our daycare is equipped with modern safety features and follows strict safety protocols.
  • Creative Learning - We believe in learning through play. Our curriculum encourages creativity, problem-solving, and emotional development.
  • Family-Centered Approach - We work closely with parents to ensure we meet every child's individual needs and foster open communication.

Our Programs

We offer a range of early learning and childcare programs tailored to each age group, delivered by caring, qualified educators in a nurturing and stimulating environment.

  • Infant Care (Aspen) - A nurturing environment for babies with individualized care.
  • Toddler Program (Cedar) - Hands-on learning to support your toddler’s growing curiosity.
  • Pre-School (Willow) - Preparing children for kindergarten with a strong foundation in literacy and numeracy.
  • Four to Pre-Kindergarten (Maple) - Advanced skills development for children ready for the next educational step.
  • Kindergarten to Out-Of-School Care - Fun and learning after school, with activities and support for schoolwork.
